Mount Olympus

Image: Mount Olympus

Description

Mount Olympus is the highest mountain in Greece. It's part of the Olympus massif, located near the Thermaic Gulf of the Aegean Sea.

Stats:

  • Height: 2,917.727 meters
  • Location: Located on the border between the regions of Thessaly and Macedonia in Greece.It features 52 peaks and deep gorges, with a rugged and majestic landscape.
  • Mythology: In Greek mythology, Mount Olympus was considered the abode of the gods. It was believed to be the dwelling place of the Olympian deities, including Zeus, the king of gods.
  • Biodiversity: Mount Olympus is a National Park and a World Biosphere Reserve.
