Mount Cook

Image: Mount Cook

Description

Aoraki / Mount Cook is the highest mountain in New Zealand, located in the Southern Alps on the South Island.

Stats:

  • Height: 3,724 meters
  • Summits: It consists of three summits: the Low Peak (3,593 m), the Middle Peak (3,717 m), and the High Peak.
  • Location: Aoraki / Mount Cook National Park in the Canterbury Region is home to this majestic peak. The park contains over 140 peaks above 2,000 meters and 72 named glaciers.
  • First Ascent: In 1894 by Tom Fyfe, George Graham, and Jack Clarke.
  • Hike Difficulty: The climb remains a challenging endeavor due to the ever-changing conditions.
  • Type of Volcano:Stratovolcano (composite volcano)
