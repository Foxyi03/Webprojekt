Maya Pyramid

Image: Maya Pyramid

Description

The Maya pyramids are remarkable structures built by the ancient Maya civilization in the jungles of Central America between 200 BCE and 900 CE. Unlike the straight-sided Egyptian pyramids, Maya pyramids have stepped sides, allowing people to ascend to the top. These stepped designs often served ceremonial purposes, including sacrificial rituals and worship of gods.

Stats:

  • Tikal:At the ruins of the ancient Maya city of Tikal (now a national park in Guatemala), a whole complex of pyramids can be found. These pyramids were the tallest structures in the Americas for around 1,000 years. Tikal's population reached 90,000 people despite its challenging swampland environment.
