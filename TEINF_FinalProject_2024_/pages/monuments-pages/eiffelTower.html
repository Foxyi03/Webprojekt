Eiffel Tower

Image: Eiffel Tower

Description

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron structure located in Paris, France. Designed and built by Gustave Eiffel between 1887 and 1889, it stands as one of the most famous landmarks in the world.

Stats:

  • Height: The tower stands 330 meters tall, equivalent to an 81-storey building.
  • Width: The base of the tower measures 410 feet on each side.
  • Weight: The metal frame weighs 7,300 tons, and the total weight (including other components) is 10,100 tons.
  • Rivets: It took approximately 2.5 million rivets to construct the Eiffel Tower.
  • Steps: The tower has 108 stories with a total of 1,710 steps.
  • Nickname:The French affectionally call it "La Dame en Fer", which means "The Iron Lady".
